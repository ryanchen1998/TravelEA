About

Travel EA is your one-stop shop for travel inspiration.

Trips

By selecting a location on the map, you can add a unique destination. Add a primary photo to a destination as well as linking relevant photos. To share your creation with the world, simply make the destination public. From all public destination you can create a custom trip, specifying departure and arrival dates. You can even copy public trips from other people who you follow. This adds it to your profile, and you can make changes.

Social

On the home-page you will find you main news feed, containing all public activity of those you follow. You can find new people to follow though the people tab, or using the explore version of the news feed. On a users profile, you will see there personal news feed. If you like what you see, you can follow them, adding this content to your main feed. The more people you follow, the more dynamic your news feed, and the more inspiration.

More

Follow other travellers and destinations to put a spark back into your travel planning.

Features such as tags and traveller types help you to find relevant content. Photos add interest to a destination and let you make your profile unique and appealing. With a high-end display and intuitive navigation, TravelEA is a pleasure to use.
