About

Travel EA is your one-stop shop for travel inspiration.

Follow other travellers and destinations to put a spark back into your travel planning. Through a collection of convenient news feeds across the application, you can view changes and keep up to date. You can create personalised trips from an extensive collection of destinations. Don’t see the destination you want? Simply add it yourself! If someone has a trip that’s appeals, you can copy it to your profile and adjust it to suit you. Features such as tags and traveller types help you to find relevant content. Photos add interest to a destination and let you make your profile unique and appealing. With a high-end display and intuitive navigation, TravelEA is a pleasure to use.
